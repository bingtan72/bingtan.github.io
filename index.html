Welcome to my homepage! In January 2024, Bing Tan joined the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Southwest University as an Associate Professor. In December 2023, he received his Ph.D. from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China under the co-supervision of Prof. Songxiao Li, Prof. Xiaolong Qin, and Prof. Yi-bin Xiao.
