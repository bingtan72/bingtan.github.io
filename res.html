Biography

I am a first-year PhD candidate in Mathematical Optimization at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, co-advised by Prof. Songxiao Li and Prof. Xiaolong Qin. Prior to that, I received a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from Southwest Petroleum University. My research interests lie at the intersection of optimization and image processing. I currently work on designing fast and practical algorithms for optimization problems in Hilbert spaces.
