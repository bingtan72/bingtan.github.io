Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. candidate in Mathematical Optimization at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, advised by Songxiao Li and Xiaolong Qin. Prior to that, I received a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from Southwest Petroleum University. My research interests lie at the intersection of optimization and machine learning. I currently work on designing fast and practical algorithms for convex optimization.
